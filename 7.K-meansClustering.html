  • L mt trong nhng thut ton hc khng gim st (unsupervised learning) n gin v ph bin nht gii quyt vn phn cm.

2.2 Hàm mất mát và phương pháp tối ưu¶

2.2.1 Hàm mất mát¶

Gi s ta c $N$ im d liu l $X=[x_1,x_2,...,x_N]$ v iu ta mun l phn $N$ im d liu thnh $K$ cm khc nhau. iu chng ta cn tm l cc trng tm $m_1, m_2,...,m_k$ ca $K$ v gn nhn tng im d liu vo cc cm tng ng.

Vi mi im d liu $x_i$ t $y_i=[y_{i1},y_{i2},...,y_{iK}]$ l vect nhn, trong nu $x_i$ thuc v cm th $k$ th $y_{ik}=1$ v $y_{ij}=0$ vi $\forall i \neq k$. $\implies \begin{cases} y_{ik} \in \{0,1\}\\ \sum_{j=1}^Ky_{ij}=1 \end{cases} \quad (1)$

V d: nu im d liu c vect nhn l $[1,0,0,...,0]$ th n s thuc vo cm th 1

Cch m ha nhn ca d liu th ny c gi l biu din one-hot (mt cch biu din kh ph bin trong hc my)

Nu ta coi $m_k$ l trng tm ca cm th $k$ th tt c cc im d liu $x_i$ c phn vo cm $k$ s c khong cch n trng tm l $||x_i-m_k||_2^2$.

iu ta mun l khong cch ny phi nh nht v v $x_i$ c phn vo cm $k$ nn $y_{ik}=1$ v $y_{ij}=0$ vi $\forall i \neq k$. Khi khong cch ca mi im n trng tm ca n s c vit li nh sau: $$y_{ik}||x_i-m_k||_2^2$$ V ng vi $K$ cm th ta c tng khong cch s l: $$\sum_{j=1}^Ky_{ij}||x_i-m_j||^2_2$$ T th ta suy ra khong cch ca ton b d liu n cc trng tm l: $$L(Y,M)=\sum_{i=0}^N\sum_{j=1}^Ky_{ij}||x_i-m_j||^2_2$$ Trong $Y=[y_1,y_2,...,y_N]$ l ma trn to bi cc vect nhn ca tng im d liu, $M=[m_1,m_2,...,m_k]$ l ma trn to bi cc trng tm ca $K$ cm cn phn chia.

Hm s $L(Y,M)$ trn l hm mt mt ca thut ton K-means tha iu kin $(1)$.

Tm li iu ta cn l ti bi ton: $$Y,M=\arg \min_{Y,M}\sum_{i=0}^N\sum_{j=1}^Ky_{ij}||x_i-m_j||^2_2 \quad (2)$$

Vi iu kin: $\begin{cases} y_{ik} \in \{0,1\}\\ \sum_{j=1}^Ky_{ij}=1 \end{cases}$

2.2.1 Phương pháp tối ưu¶

Mt cch n gin tm nghim ti u cho phng trnh $(2)$ l ta s thay phin tm $Y$ v $M$ trong khi c nh bin cn li. y chnh cng chnh l thut ton K-means ta nu trn.

C nh $M$, gii tm $Y$

Gi s tm c cc trng tm, hy tm cc vect nhn hm mt mt t gi tr nh nht.

iu ny tng ng vi tm cm tng ng cho mi im d liu.

Khi cc cm c nh th bi ton ca ta c chia nh ra thnh tm vect nhn cho tng im d liu $x_i$ nh sau:$$y_i=\arg\min_{y_i}\sum_{i=1}^Ky_{ij}||x_i-m_j||_2^2 \quad (3)$$

iu kin: $\begin{cases} y_{ik} \in \{0,1\}\\ \sum_{j=1}^Ky_{ij}=1 \end{cases}$

V ch c mt phn t ca vect nhn $y_i$ bng $1$ nn phng trnh (3) c vit li l: $$j=\arg\min_j||x_i-m_j||_2^2$$

Ta c $||x_i-m_j||_2^2$ chnh l bnh phng khong cch ca $x_i$ n $m_j$ v vy ta c th thy $x_i$ s thuc vo cm c tm gn n nht, t suy ra vect nhn ca $x_i$.

C nh $Y$, gii tm $M$

Gi s tm c cm cho tng im, hy tm trng tm mi cho mi cm hm mt mt t gi tr nh nht.

Mt khi chng ta xc nh c vect nhn cho tng im d liu, bi ton tm trng tm cho mi cm c rt gn thnh: $$l(m_j)=\arg \min_{m_j}\sum_{i=1}^Ny_{ij}||x_i-m_j||^2_2 \quad (3)$$ Ta c th thy phng trnh $(3)$ l mt hm lin tc v c o hm ti mi im nn ta c th tm nghim bng cch gii phng trnh o hm bng $0$ .

Ly o hm theo bin $m_j$ ta c: $$\frac{\partial l(m_j)}{\partial m_j}=2\sum_{i=1}^Ny_{ij}(m_j-x_i)$$

Gii phng trnh o hm bng $0$ ta c: $$m_j=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^Ny_{ij}x_j}{\sum_{i=1}^Ny_{ij}}$$ Nh ta thy th nghim l trung bnh ca cc phn t thuc mi cm $j$. Ci tn K-means cng xut pht t y.

def kmeans_display(X, label):
    K = np.amax(label) + 1
    X0 = X[label == 0, :]
    X1 = X[label == 1, :]
    X2 = X[label == 2, :]
    
    plt.plot(X0[:, 0], X0[:, 1], 's', markersize = 4, alpha = .8)
    plt.plot(X1[:, 0], X1[:, 1], 'o', markersize = 4, alpha = .8)
    plt.plot(X2[:, 0], X2[:, 1], 'v', markersize = 4, alpha = .8)

    plt.axis('equal')
    plt.plot()
    plt.show()
